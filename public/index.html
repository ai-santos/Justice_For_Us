Connecting the Revolution

"We urgently need to bring to our communities the limitless capacity to love, serve and create for and with each other." - Grace Lee Boggs, revolutionary activist and teacher from Detroit.
The personal is political. Justice For Us seeks to build solidarity and authentic community by using the power of technology to efficiently connect our lives in the myriad of ways that allow us to grow interdependently. This site was built by activists for activists, with our needs in mind and to break free from social media sites whose purpose is to grow capital rather than community.
